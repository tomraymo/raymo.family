Mitchell Raymo

Mitchell Raymo was born 19 February 1813. He married Laura Perry on 1 January 1835. She was born 24 April 1816 and died 9 Jul 1840.[1]

Children of Mitchell Raymo and Laura Perry:

  1. Elizabeth Raymo, b. 9 November 1835.
  2. Rhody Ann Raymo, b. 23 September 1837.
