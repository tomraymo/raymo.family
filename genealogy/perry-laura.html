Laura Perry

Laura Perry was born 24 April 1816 and died 9 Jul 1840. She married Mitchell Raymo on 1 January 1835.[1]

Children of Laura Perry and Mitchell Raymo:

  1. Elizabeth Raymo, b. 9 November 1835.
  2. Rhody Ann Raymo, b. 23 September 1837.
